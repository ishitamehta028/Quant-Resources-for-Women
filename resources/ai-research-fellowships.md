# 25 AI Research Fellowships — Structured Tracker

The structured, trackable companion to the Substack article *"25 AI Research Fellowships You Should Actually Apply For."* Same 25 programs, organized as a table you can actually filter and act on.

Last major review of this file: 2026-06-21. **Deadlines and stipend amounts in this field change constantly** — treat the "Timing" column as directional, not exact, and always confirm on the official page before applying. Entries marked **[verify link]** need a contributor to confirm the official deep-link — see [`CONTRIBUTING.md`](../CONTRIBUTING.md).
